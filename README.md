Customer Churn Analysis:

Overview- This project analyzes customer churn to identify patterns and factors that lead to customer loss. The goal is to provide insights that help improve customer retention and business decision-making.

Objectives- 1. Measure overall churn rate 2. Identify high-risk customer segments 3. Analyze churn based on contract type, tenure, and monthly charges

Tools & Technologies-
Excel – Data cleaning and preparation
SQLite – Data analysis using SQL
Tableau – Data visualization and dashboarding

Key Insights- 1. Customers on month-to-month contracts have higher churn, 2. New customers are more likely to leave, 3. Higher monthly charges are associated with increased churn

Recommendations- 1. Encourage long-term contracts, 2. Improve onboarding experience for new customers, 3. Optimize pricing strategies for high-cost plans
